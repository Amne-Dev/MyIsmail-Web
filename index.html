FAQs

Here are some of the most frequently asked questions about our school:

What are the school hours?

Our school operates from 8:00 AM to 6:00 PM with a break occuring from 12:00 PM to 2:00 PM, Monday through Friday. Special schedules may apply on holidays or during events.

How do I apply for admission?

To apply, please visit our Administration, and submit all required documents.

What activities are offered?

We offer a wide range of activities including sports (basketball, Volleyball, Handball), music, Art, debate club, and various student organizations.

How can I get involved with the school community?

Students and parents can get involved through activities, volunteering opportunities, and attending school events. Look for upcoming events on our facebook page.

What is the dress code?

Our dress code encourages students to wear modest clothing appropriate for a learning environment. Specific guidelines on dress will be provided at the beginning of each academic year.

How can I contact a teacher?

Teachers can be contacted directly after class or through email. You may also schedule parent-teacher conferences through the school office.

What support services are available for students?

We offer a range of support services including counseling, academic tutoring and career guidance.

How does the school handle bullying?

The school has a strict anti-bullying policy. Any instances of bullying are taken seriously and will be investigated. Students are encouraged to report bullying incidents to a teacher, counselor, or administrator.

Can I bring my own device to school?

Yes, students are allowed to bring their own devices for educational purposes. Please refer to the school’s Acceptable Use Policy for guidelines on device use in classrooms.

How can I stay updated with school news?

You can stay updated by checking the school website, following us on facebook.

What happens if I miss a class?

If a student misses a class, it is their responsibility to catch up on missed assignments and lessons. They can speak with the teacher to get materials or attend after-school tutoring if needed.

How do I register for school events or activities?

Registration for school events and activities can typically be done in person at the school office.

Can I switch classes if I am struggling with a subject?

Yes, students can discuss class changes with their counselor if they are having difficulty in a subject. It’s important to seek help early and try tutoring options before considering a change.

What should I do if I lose my school ID or any personal belongings?

Lost IDs and belongings can be reported to the school office. We keep a lost and found section where items are stored for retrieval.
